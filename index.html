What Mise Does

Everything your bakery needs, in one place

Mise connects your ingredients, your recipes, and your sales — so you always know where you stand.

Step 1

Log your ingredients

Add your pantry items with price, quantity, and unit. Enter your gram-to-cup and gram-to-spoon conversions once — Mise handles all the math from there.

Inventory tracking

Step 2

Build your recipes — privately

Select your ingredients and add measurements. Mise instantly calculates the exact cost per batch and per piece. Your recipes stay completely private.
